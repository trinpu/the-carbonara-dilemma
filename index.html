The Carbonara Dilemma

An existential question: What is the orignial recipe for the famous pasta alla carbonara?

Well, I'm not a time-travelling chef so I can't go back in time to check. But, as an Italian, I can share with you the version that got me the highest number of "wow that blew my mind!".

P.S. before be begin, here is a little secret: just like this HTML page, the secret to a good carbonara is too keep things simple!.

The Ingredients

(NOTE: This recipie serves two people)

Here are the ingredients

  • 160g of Pasta
  • 60g of Pecorino Romano
  • 20g of Parmigiano Reggiano
  • 4 eggs
  • 100g of Guanciale
  • Salt and pepper
  • AND FOR THE LOVE OF GOD, NO CREAM!
